How splitting works

每次 shared expense 都係先由 shared pool 扣數；participants 只係用嚟記錄嗰筆消費由邊幾個人分攤。

例如 Lunch / Taxi HK$240，得 3 個人 join：
每人 share = HK$80。

Every expense record deducts from the shared pool first. Participants are only used to calculate who consumed that expense. Pool balance = total deposits - total shared expenses。
